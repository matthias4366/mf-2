Import ingredient from FoodData Central

You can import ingredient data from FoodData Central. It is an official website from the United States Department of Agriculture. Go to https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/fdc-app.html#/ and search for the desired ingredient. Once you have found the ingredient, copy the FDC ID number (e.g. 335912), paste it in the form below and click 'Add this ingredient to my ingredients'.
